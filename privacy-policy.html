Privacy Policy for Extra Bookmarks

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

Data Collection

Extra Bookmarks does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains local to your browser.

What Data is Stored

  • Bookmark URLs and titles you create
  • Custom bar configurations (number of bars, names)
  • Display preferences (scale, spacing, opacity, collapsed state)

Where Data is Stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and optionally synced across your devices through Chrome Sync (if enabled).

Data Usage

  • Data is used solely to display and manage your custom bookmark bars
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is collected
  • No data is shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

The extension uses Google's favicon service (https://www.google.com/s2/favicons) to display website icons. This is a standard image request and does not share your browsing data.

Permissions

  • Storage: Saves your bookmarks and settings locally
  • Bookmarks: Allows interaction with browser bookmarks
  • Host Permissions: Displays bookmark bars on all web pages

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes by updating this policy and the effective date.
